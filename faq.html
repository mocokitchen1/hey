Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handmade aprons and delivery.

How long does delivery take?

For Paxi delivery, it typically takes 7-10 working days. In-store collection in Pietermaritzburg is usually ready within 2-3 working days after payment confirmation.

How do I pay for my order?

We currently accept EFT (Bank Transfer) and Cash on Collection. Bank details are provided on the contact page and during checkout. Please use your Order ID as the reference.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We have a permanent bulk deal for our Basic Aprons: 10 aprons for R700 (saving you R100). This discount is automatically applied at checkout.

Can I request custom sizes?

Absolutely. When placing your order, use the "Special Measurements / Notes" section to provide specific requirements. We pride ourselves on the handmade nature of our products.
