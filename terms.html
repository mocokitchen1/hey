Terms of Service & Privacy

Please read our policies carefully before ordering.

1. Ordering & Payments

All orders placed on our website are subject to availability. Payments must be made via EFT or Cash on Collection before the order is processed. Please use your Order ID as the reference for EFT payments to avoid delays.

2. Delivery & Collections

We use Paxi for standard nationwide delivery (7-10 working days). In-store collections are available at our Pietermaritzburg location. We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party delivery services.

3. Handmade Quality

Our products are handmade. As such, minor variations in stitching or color may occur. We pride ourselves on the unique quality of every piece we produce.

4. Privacy & Data

We respect your privacy. Your contact details and delivery information are stored securely and will only be used to process your order and provide customer support. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Returns & Exchanges

Due to the handmade nature of our items, we only offer returns or exchanges for manufacturing defects. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order if you encounter any issues.
